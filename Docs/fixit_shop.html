Shop / Workshop — What they do
Shops browse a filtered feed of customer requests and place bids with a price, availability, and message. They never post — only bid. When a customer accepts a bid, a direct in-app chat opens. The shop feed is filtered by their service type, car makes, categories, and distance radius set during signup.
